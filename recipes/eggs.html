Fried Eggs

Description

Sunny side up fried eggs with tomatoes and veggies.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Veggies
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack eggs and place in a bowl
  2. Add salt and pepper and mix it up
  3. Place it in fryinmg pan
  4. Add Tomatoes and veggies and let it fry a little
