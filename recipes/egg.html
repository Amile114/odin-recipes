Fry Eggs

Index Chowmein

Frying eggs is one of the simplest meal anyone can whip up with just a few simple ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Oil

Those are the basic of the basic ingredients required for this. Of course you can add more and customize it however you like it

Simple steps

  1. Put the oil in the frying pan
  2. Crack the egg and place it in the hot oil
  3. Let it fry to your liking
